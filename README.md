# Boulder Green Commute Guide

A comprehensive guide to sustainable commuting for Boulder, Colorado residents.

## Walking Route: Oak Avenue to 1800 Broadway
- **Distance:** 4.0 km (2.5 miles)
- **Duration:** ~7 minutes
- **Route:** Oak Ave → 19th St → 20th St → Pearl St → 18th St → Canyon Blvd

## Biking Route: Oak Avenue to 1800 Broadway
- **Distance:** 4.0 km (2.5 miles)  
- **Duration:** ~7 minutes
- **Route:** Oak Ave → 19th St → 20th St → Pearl St → 18th St → Canyon Blvd

## Environmental Impact
- CO₂ saved per trip: ~0.5 kg vs. driving
- Annual savings (5 days/week): ~12,000 kg CO₂
- Health benefits: cardio fitness, reduced stress

## Boulder Sustainable commuting Tips
1. Use bike lanes on 19th and 20th Streets
2. Park your bike at Pearl Street Mall racks
3. Enjoy the Flatirons views on Canyon Boulevard
4. Combine with RTD bus for longer distances
5. Check weather and dress in layers

*Made for a greener Boulder!*
